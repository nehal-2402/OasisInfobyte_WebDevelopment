About the SpaceCraft Club

Welcome to the SpaceCraft Club! We are a group of enthusiasts who are passionate about space and rocket science and exploring the boundaries of space. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of scientists and engineers by providing opportunities for hands-on learning and experimentation. SpaceCraft club refers to a group of people who are interested in rocket science and aerospace technology. The members of these clubs typically participate in various activities related to rocketry, such as building and launching model rockets, conducting experiments and simulations, learning about rocket propulsion systems, and exploring space and aeronautical engineering. SpaceCraft clubs also provide opportunities for members to learn from each other and share their knowledge and experiences.Join us today to start your journey into the exciting world of SpaceCraft.

Events By SpaceCraft Club

  • Model Rocket Launches: One of the most common events in a rocketry club is a model rocket launch. Participants can build and launch model rockets, either from scratch or from kits, and compare their performance. This is a great opportunity to learn about rocket design and aerodynamics.
  • Workshops: Workshops can be organized to teach members about various aspects of spacecraft , such as rocket propulsion systems, electronics, or aerodynamics. These workshops can be conducted by club members or outside experts.

  • Simulations: Rocketry club members can participate in computer simulations of rocket launches, which can be used to learn about rocket behavior and performance.
  • Guest Speakers: Rocketry clubs can invite guest speakers to talk about their experiences in the field, such as working for a rocket company or conducting research.
  • Field Trips: Field trips to aerospace and space-related facilities, such as ISRO centers or aerospace museums, can provide opportunities for members to learn about the latest advancements in rocketry and space exploration.

Recruitment Process

  • The Spacecraft Club of Pune University is excited to announce its recruitment for first-year students! We are looking for enthusiastic and motivated individuals who are passionate about rocket science and aerospace technology. If you have an interest in rockets and space exploration and would like to learn more about these fields, then the Spacecraft Club is the perfect place for you!
  • As a member of the Spacecraft Club, you will have the opportunity to participate in a variety of events, workshops, and competitions, as well as engage in hands-on learning experiences. You will also have the chance to interact with other students who share your interests, as well as industry professionals and experts in the field.
  • To be considered for membership, you must be a first-year student at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology and have an interest in rocketry and aerospace technology. There are no prerequisites or prior experience required all levels of experience are welcome!
  • If you are interested in joining the Rocketry Club, please fill out the membership application form and provide a brief statement of your interests and goals. Our membership committee will review your application and get back to you with more information.
  • Don't miss this exciting opportunity to be a part of a dynamic and passionate community of students and professionals! Join the Spacecraft club and explore the exciting world of rockets and space!
